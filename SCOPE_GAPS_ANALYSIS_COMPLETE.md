## Root Cause Analysis

### Possible Causes for Variances:

1. **Different Measurement Basis**
   - Suppliers may have measured from different drawing sets
   - Fire engineer's schedule vs electrical/services drawings discrepancy
   - Different interpretation of "penetration" vs "cable" counts

2. **Scope Interpretation**
   - Suppliers may have excluded certain areas/zones
   - Different understanding of "each" (per penetration vs per cable)
   - Confusion between service types (data vs electrical cables)

3. **Itemization Method**
   - Some suppliers group items differently
   - Bundled penetrations vs individual cable counts
   - Service tray vs individual cables within trays

4. **Missing Information**
   - Incomplete fire engineer's schedule provided to tenderers
   - Missing service coordination drawings
   - Unclear specification of FRR requirements per location
