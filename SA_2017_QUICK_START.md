# SA-2017 Quick Start Guide

## What's New?

SA-2017 Subcontract Agreement is now integrated into **Step 5: Subcontractor Onboarding** as the **4th sub-step**.

## How to Access

1. Open **Contract Manager** for any project
2. Navigate to **Step 5: Subcontractor Onboarding** (top workflow bar)
3. Click **Sub-step 4: Sub-Contract Agreement** (bottom right of sub-step bar)

## Quick Actions

### Create New Agreement
1. Click **"Create SA-2017 Agreement"** button
2. Agreement auto-creates with number (SA-0001, SA-0002, etc.)
3. Card appears with status "Draft"

### Edit Agreement
1. Click **"Open Agreement"** button
2. Full SA-2017 workflow opens in new window
3. Fill in 80+ fields across 6 sections
4. Save draft or complete

### View Status
- **Draft** (gray badge) = In progress
- **In Review** (blue badge) = Submitted for review
- **Completed** (green badge) = Done ✓

### Export PDF
- Available when status = "Completed"
- Click "Export PDF" button
- Download formal SA-2017 document

## Completion Tracking

## Common Questions

### Q: Can I create multiple agreements?
**A:** Yes, but Step 5 shows the most recent one.

### Q: What if I don't have award information?
**A:** Complete the award process first. SA-2017 requires approved subcontractor details.

### Q: Why does it open in a new window?
**A:** SA-2017 is a complex 80+ field form. Separate window provides better UX and allows you to reference Contract Manager while filling it out.

### Q: Can I go back and edit?
**A:** Yes, click "Open Agreement" anytime (unless locked).

### Q: What happens when I lock an agreement?
**A:** It becomes view-only. A warning displays in Step 5.
